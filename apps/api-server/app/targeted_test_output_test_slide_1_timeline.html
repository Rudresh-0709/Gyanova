The Evolution of AI

1

The journey of Artificial Intelligence began in the 1950s with Alan Turing's philosophical question: 'Can machines think?'.

2

In the 1980s, Expert Systems used rule-based logic to tackle specific problems but faced challenges in scalability.

3

A breakthrough in 2012 came with AlexNet, showcasing the power of Deep Learning in image recognition.

4

Today, Generative AI models like Transformers create art, code, and text, revolutionizing human interaction with information.

The pace of AI development is accelerating faster than ever before.
